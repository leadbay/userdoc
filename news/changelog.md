# Changelog

Leadbay ships updates every week. Below are the major milestones.

---

## February 2026

- **Enriched contacts export**: CSV export now includes enriched contacts with company, last name, first name, phone number, email, job title, and LinkedIn profile.
- **Bulk contact enrichment**: select multiple leads from any tab, choose target job titles, and enrich contacts in batch.
- **Select-all**: bulk operations now support selecting all leads matching your current filters.

## January 2026

- **Usage-based pricing**: no leads visited in a month = next month billed €0. No commitment required.
- **8-step guided onboarding**: new in-app walkthrough for first-time users, covering all features and tabs hands-on.
- **Activate tab classifications**: leads in Activate are now sorted into Activatable, On hold, and Completed.
- **Bulk status change**: set status for multiple leads at once.
- **LinkedIn Ads integration**: export leads to LinkedIn Ads audiences via Zapier.

## October 2025

- **Redesigned lead profile**: qualification question answers shown with color indicators (positive / neutral / negative).
- **"Predicted Won" label**: displayed on leads very close to your won deal profile.
- **AI-generated next steps**: the model now suggests recommended approach angles for each lead.

## September 2025

- **Self-serve signup**: try Leadbay for free directly from the website — no demo, no complex integration.
- **AI Chat in lead profiles**: prepare meetings and generate summaries with context-aware AI, right inside the lead profile.
- **AI Assistant with qualification questions**: configure up to 5 strategic questions that the AI answers automatically for every lead.
- **Auto field detection for imports**: Leadbay now detects fields and values automatically when importing files.

## July 2025

- **Activate tab**: dedicated workspace for daily prospecting, showing liked leads ranked by relevance with next-step actions.
- **Redesigned lead profile**: new tabbed layout with Company, Contacts, Notes, History, and Subsidiaries.
- **AI summary**: automatic lead overview visible in the profile for quick context.
- **Prospecting actions**: log activity with one click — Still chasing, Meeting planned, Could not reach, Not interested.
- **Web Fetch**: pull fresh data from a lead's website (company profile, key people, technologies).
- **"Liked leads" replaces "Saved leads"**: terminology update for clarity.
- **Notifications**: receive alerts when contact enrichments complete (bell icon, top-right).

## April 2025

- **Contact enrichment**: enrich contacts with emails and phone numbers via FullEnrich partnership (~80% email coverage, ~65% phone coverage).

## March 2025

- **Mobile app**: Leadbay available as a PWA for iPhone and Android — install in under a minute.

## February 2025

- **Manager Dashboard**: managers can track team prospecting performance; individual reps can review their own activity.
- **French language**: the app auto-detects browser language and displays in French when appropriate.

## December 2024

- **Lead descriptions in list**: company descriptions visible directly in the lead table without opening each profile.
- **Canton/city filter**: distinguish between administrative regions and cities in location filters.
- **Timeline**: smart recommendations based on user interactions — clicks, likes, exports.

## October 2024

- **Quick like/dislike**: thumbs up/down buttons directly in lead list rows for faster feedback.

## September 2024

- **Map view**: visualize selected leads on a geographic map, share the link, or open it on your phone for field visits.

## July 2024

- **Timeline feature**: AI-generated recommendations that learn from everything you do on Leadbay.

## May 2024

- **Refreshing Discovery**: Discover becomes a continuous flow of incoming leads. New leads marked with a green dot. Stale leads automatically cycled out.

## April 2024

- **Lenses**: saved filter configurations for different market segments. Keep separate parameters for different products, territories, or verticals.

## February 2024

- **Zapier connector**: import and export leads between Leadbay and your CRM via Zapier.
- **Scoring overhaul**: 0–99 scale based on AI-computed similarity to your won deals.

## January 2024

- **AI-generated descriptions**: every lead gets an auto-generated summary of its products, services, and market.
- **Discover and Monitor modes**: two views — AI recommendations (Discover) and pipeline tracking (Monitor).
- **Status predictions**: predicted outcomes shown for leads not yet won or lost.
